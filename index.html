About Me

I received the B.S. degree in computer science and technology from Hunan Normal University, Changsha, China in 2004 and the M.S. degrees in computer technology from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China in 2008. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer software and theory from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China in 2016. I am an associate professor in Jiujiang University, China. My research interests include evolutionary algorithm and its real-word applications.
